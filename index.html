TYPES OF PETS IN OUR STORE

Teacup puppy

Teacup Puppy: The Perfect Pocket-Sized Companion! Teacup puppies are irresistibly adorable and incredibly affectionate little companions, typically weighing under 5 pounds. Known for their tiny size and big personalities, these pups are perfect for those who want a loyal friend without needing a lot of space. Their playful nature and loving demeanor make them ideal for families, singles, or seniors alike. With proper care and training, teacup puppies can bring endless joy and companionship to your life!

Siberian Husky

Siberian Huskies are strikingly beautiful, with their iconic blue or multicolored eyes and thick double coats. Known for their friendly and outgoing nature, these intelligent and energetic dogs thrive on exercise and companionship. Originally bred as sled dogs, Huskies are playful, social, and great with families, making them the perfect adventure buddy. With a strong pack mentality, they love being part of a family and enjoy activities like running, hiking, and playing in the snow.

Shih Tzu

The Shih Tzu is a small, Long-haired breed of dog known for it's luxurios, flowing coat and sweet, playful personality. They are considered toy dogs and are known for their gentle, affectionate nature. Shih Tzu makes excellent companions and are often described as "little lions" due to their distinctive facial features. They are intelligent and eager to please making them relatively easy to train.

Rovorovski Dwarf Hamster

The Roborovski dwarf hamster is a small, lively pet known for its playful and curious nature. Typically measuring around 4 inches in length, these hamsters have a sandy or light brown coat with distinctive white markings on their faces and bellies. They are social creatures that thrive in pairs or small groups, making them a delightful addition to any home. With their energetic antics and gentle demeanor, Roborovski dwarf hamsters are perfect for both novice and experienced pet owners.
